## Product Overview

Need a meditation timer that stays out of your way? Quiet is a mindfulness bell timer I built for my own practice - no guided sessions, no distractions. Just a simple timer that lets you sit with your thoughts.

I wanted something minimal: set your timer, breathe, and let gentle bells mark the beginning and end. That's it.

What's included:

- Customizable timer with start/end bells
- Optional ambient sounds (rain, waves, forest)
- Interval bells to help you stay present
- Session history synced via iCloud
- Apple Health integration
- Stats tracking (streaks, total time)
- No ads, no tracking, no data collection

Quiet is designed for people who just want to meditate without a lot of extra stuff. Everything stays private on your device. Your practice is yours alone.

I made this app because I needed it. If you're looking for a straightforward mindfulness bell timer, maybe it'll work for you too.
