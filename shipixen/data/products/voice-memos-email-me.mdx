## Product Overview

AI Voice Memos is designed to be the fastest way to record your ideas, thoughts, tasks, and follow-ups when you're on the go.

It is the easiest and fastest way to email something to yourself so you will never forget things again. Ever.

CORE FEATURES AND BENEFITS
– Send notes to your email in one tap
– Send audio notes with transcriptions
– Send images from your Camera or Library in one tap
– Send your clipboard content in one tap
– Supports multiple recipients with multiple email addresses
– Supports Widgets
– Supports iOS App Extensions
– Universal App for iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Vision Pro
– Multiple custom App icons and themes
– Siri and Shortcuts support
– Option to use your own SMTP Configuration

AI TOOLS
– Summarize any note in one tap
– Turn a note into a task list automatically
– Translate a note into another language
– Edit any AI result with a prompt before regenerating over your changes

TRANSCRIPTION
– Transcribe your own audio notes automatically
– Transcribe WhatsApp voice notes by sharing them into the app
– Transcribe Apple Voice Memos by sharing them into the app
– On-device transcription support

SHARE EXTENSION / ACTION EXTENSION
– Share a voice note to transcribe it in one tap
– Share a link from Safari in one tap
– Share text from a website in one tap
– Share an image in one tap from the Action Extension
– Share extension supports major App Store apps

APPLE WATCH
– Standalone Watch App
– Watch App Complications for every Complication type
– Send text notes in one tap
– Send audio notes in one tap
– Send audio recordings in one tap
– Notes sent from your Watch appear in My Notes on your iPhone

MAC APP
– Standalone macOS App
– Integrates as a macOS menu bar icon
– Supports macOS Widgets
– Supports macOS Share Extension

EASY TO CONFIGURE
– Set predefined email addresses
– Set a predefined subject prefix if desired
– Set a predefined email signature if desired
– Set an email alias
– Set the reply-to email address

ACCESSIBILITY
– Accessibility labels app-wide for VoiceOver
– Supports dynamic text sizing
– Supports sounds and vibration feedback
– Supports dark mode
– Custom CoreHaptics for precise haptics
– Reduce Motion accessibility checks within the app, which automatically reduce motion

IMPORTANT
– Send an unlimited number of emails
– Notes are delivered to your email inbox instantly
– No data collected or shared with third parties, at all
– AI Voice Memos supports two URL schemes:
  EmailMe://?message=Hello
  emailme://hello
