## Product Overview

Walk Mate - Daily Walking Route Generator

Transform your daily walks from routine to remarkable with Walk Mate, your personal walking route generator. Whether you're a morning stroller, lunch break wanderer, or evening explorer, WalkMate creates fresh circular routes that help you rediscover your neighborhood one step at a time.

Features

1. Daily Route Generator
Each day brings three new walking adventures, carefully crafted to start and end at your doorstep. No more walking the same path twice unless you choose to.

2. Medal Collection System
Turn every walk into a treasure hunt. Collect medals scattered along your routes as you explore your neighborhood. Each medal you discover adds to your achievement streak and unlocks new milestones. Build your collection and watch your progress grow with every step.

3. Compete with Friends & Family
Challenge the people who matter most. Invite friends and family to join WalkMate and compete on the leaderboard based on medals collected. See who can discover the most routes, maintain the longest streak, or collect the most medals each week. Healthy competition makes consistency easier.

4. Smart Safety Features
WalkMate prioritizes well-lit streets during evening walks and residential areas during quiet hours, helping you stay safe while exploring.

5. Personalized Experience
Set your preferred walking distance, pace, and route type. Whether you want a quick 15-minute circuit or an hour-long adventure, WalkMate adapts to your schedule.

6. Routes You'll Love
Save your favorite walks for future use, and help the app learn your preferences by rating each route. WalkMate gets better at suggesting paths you'll enjoy with each walk.

7. Simple to Start
Just open the app, tap "Generate Walk," and you're ready to explore. Turn-by-turn navigation keeps you on track while you focus on enjoying your surroundings.

Download WalkMate today and turn every walk into a new adventure, right in your own neighborhood.
