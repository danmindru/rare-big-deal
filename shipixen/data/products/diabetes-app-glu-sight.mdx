## Product Overview

Managing diabetes is hard enough. Glu Sight helps you track blood glucose, insulin, and oral medications in one place, understand your patterns, and stay consistent day to day.

Whether you manage type 2, type 1, pre-diabetes, gestational diabetes, or another form of diabetes, Glu Sight gives you a clearer view of your data so you can spot trends and keep day-to-day logging organized.

＊＊ FEATURES

Built for different therapy needs

Diabetes care varies person to person. Glu Sight lets you show only the pieces you use, so logging stays quick and the screen does not feel like homework.

- Insulin on your terms. Use insulin tracking when it matches your care, or turn it off in Profile for a simpler, glucose-first experience.
- Pills without the clutter. Add oral medications with the details you actually use (form, strength, and your usual dose), then log each dose as taken or skipped so your day matches your real routine.
- Step back without losing glucose data. Turn medication tracking off in Profile when you want glucose and insulin without pill workflows; turn it back on when you are ready.

Fast daily logging
- Log glucose, insulin, and medication doses in seconds.
- Add context like meal timing (pre- or post-meal) and insulin therapy details.

Trends and stats you can use
- See averages, estimated A1C, and time in range.
- Compare trends across 7, 14, 30, and 90 days for Average Glucose, Time in Range, and Variability.
- Spot recurring patterns with clear visual charts.

Apple Health integration
- Sync glucose and insulin data from Apple Health.
- Works with data from compatible devices and apps that write to Apple Health, including Dexcom G5/G6/G7, Accu‑Chek Connect, InPen, OneTouch Verio Flex, and others.
- Supports background updates and manual sync controls.

Helpful reminders and therapy support
- Set reminders for insulin doses and oral medications.
- Stay consistent with personalized notification flows.
- Keep insulin dose reminders in view with Live Activities on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island (on supported iPhone models), with a quick path to log when it is time for your dose.

Quick actions with Siri Shortcuts
- Jump straight into logging glucose or insulin.
- Open your previous-day recap faster.

Personalized experience
- Choose mg/dL or mmol/L.
- Set your own target glucose range.
- Customize app theme and icon.
