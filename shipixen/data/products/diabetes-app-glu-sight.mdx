## Product Overview

Glu Sight – Smarter Diabetes Tracking, Clearer Insights

Managing diabetes is hard enough. Glu Sight helps you track blood glucose and insulin in one place, understand your patterns, and stay consistent day to day.

Whether you manage type 2, type 1, pre-diabetes, gestational diabetes, or another form of diabetes, Glu Sight gives you a clearer view of your data so you can make more informed decisions.


＊＊ FEATURES

Built for different therapy needs
- Track blood glucose with or without insulin tracking
- Turn insulin tracking off in Settings for a focused glucose-only experience

Fast daily logging
- Log glucose and insulin in seconds
- Add context like meal timing (pre- or post-meal) and insulin therapy details

Trends and stats you can use
- See averages, estimated A1C, and time in range
- Compare trends across 7, 14, 30, and 90 days for Average Glucose, Time in Range, and Variability
- Spot recurring patterns with clear visual charts

Apple Health integration
- Sync glucose and insulin data from Apple Health
- Works with data from compatible devices and apps that write to Apple Health, including Dexcom G5/G6/G7, Accu‑Chek Connect, InPen, OneTouch Verio Flex, and others
- Supports background updates and manual sync controls

Helpful reminders and therapy support
- Set reminders for therapy and logging routines
- Stay consistent with personalized notification flows

Quick actions with Siri Shortcuts
- Jump straight into logging glucose or insulin
- Open your previous-day recap faster

Personalized experience
- Choose mg/dL or mmol/L
- Set your own target glucose range
- Customize app theme and icon
