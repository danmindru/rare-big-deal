## Product Overview

Turn every meeting, lecture, and conversation into clear, organized notes — automatically. Just hit record: AI Meeting Notes & Recorder transcribes your audio and delivers an accurate summary with key points and action items in seconds.

No more typing while listening. Stay fully present — the app takes the notes.

RECORD & TRANSCRIBE
- Capture meetings, lectures, interviews, and voice memos in high quality
- Get fast, accurate AI transcription — no manual typing required
- Import existing audio files or even YouTube links to transcribe

AI SUMMARIES & ACTION ITEMS
- Instantly condense hour-long recordings into clear summaries
- Extract key decisions, highlights, and to-dos automatically
- Share notes with your team in one tap

MORE WAYS TO CAPTURE
- Create notes from document scans, photos, text, and web links
- Everything searchable and organized with folders and tags

BUILT FOR STUDYING TOO
- Turn lectures into quizzes and flashcards to remember more
- Generate mind maps from any note to see the big picture
- Translate notes into multiple languages with one tap

WHO IT'S FOR
- Professionals — meeting minutes, client calls, brainstorms
- Students — lectures, study notes, exam prep
- Journalists & creators — interviews, ideas on the go
