## Product Overview

Capture temporary photos and videos that won’t clutter your main library.
CheckCam is the minimalist camera for the little things you'll worry about later — kept in a separate gallery from your real photos, and gone when you no longer need them.

What Makes CheckCam Different                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         
  
  → Isolated gallery                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
  Captures live inside CheckCam, never in your Photos library.

  → Auto-delete, your call                    
  Photos and videos disappear after 1, 7, or 30 days. You pick the lifespan.

  → Pin what matters                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
  Mark items to keep them safe from auto-delete, for as long as you want.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
  → Photo and video in one gesture                      
  Tap for a photo. Press and hold for video.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
  → Grouped by session                        
  Captures bundle into 15-min, 30-min, 1-hour, or 1-day sessions — easy to see what belongs together.                                                                                                                                                                   
                                                        
  → Gentle reminders                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
  Set a quick nudge to capture something before you leave the house.

  Built For
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
  - People who double-check everything before leaving
  - Professionals taking quick visual notes                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
  - Minimalists who want a clutter-free Photos library  
  - Older adults who rely on visual confirmation

Private by Design:
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
  No cloud sync. No tracking. No data collection. No ads.
  Fully offline. EXIF and GPS stripped on every capture.
